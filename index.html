💬 How to use these guides

They're designed to be explored, shared and returned to — not read once and forgotten.

1

Explore at your pace

Each guide is interactive — tap, toggle and click to reveal what's relevant to you right now.

2

Share with family

Send the link to grandparents, partners or siblings who want to understand but don't know where to start.

3

Share with school

The behaviour and meltdowns guides are particularly useful for teachers and support staff.

4

Return when needed

Bookmark this page. Each guide is most useful in context — come back to the right one when a situation arises.
